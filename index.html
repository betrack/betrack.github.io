Betrack

The internet of Kegs.

  • Optimize keg handling to allow operating without bottlenecks.

  • Improve customer service reducing response times.

  • Track, detect and reduce missing or stolen kegs.

  • Reduce idle kegs, increasing their utilization.

  • Real-time task management for your internal and external procedures.

  • Optimize routes and transport usage by type of transport and distance.

  • Obtain statistics and feedback from beer consumption.

  • Ensure cold chain with early alerts upon inadequate levels.

Betrack is a cloud-based system for keg monitoring.

We provide you with real-time traceability of your kegs, improving the distribution process while sharing valuable information with the brewery and the point of sale.
